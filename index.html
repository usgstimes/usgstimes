School Houses

Roses

Roses stand out. So does this house. It’s dark red shows its toughness whether it’s in academics or sports. They have it all. At the end of the day they defending champs of this school. Like a rose it’s always blooming except this house never dies.

Bluebells

When we talk about flowers the ones that come to mind are lilies and roses. But deep down bluebells beat them all. They are underdogs. Calmness is their strength and chaos is their moment. If there was a house for the most hardworking it would be bluebells.

Daffodils

The reason they’re yellow is because they bleed gold. The most sport cups belongs to daffodils, because if their determination, hardwork and immense talent. Daffodilites are either scholers, athlete’s and sometimes both. The crown belongs to them.

Shamrocks

Shamrocks the is the house where talent is key. Masters at things like extracurricular as well as academics. Not just few. Most of them. Their green colour indicates their ability to thrive and stay alive despite the challenges thrown at them. A mix of calmness and aggression is deadly in this house.
